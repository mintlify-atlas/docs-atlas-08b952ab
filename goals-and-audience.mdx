## Learning Goals

The primary goal of this 90-day plan is to transform you from a beginner into a confident cybersecurity practitioner with hands-on skills and theoretical knowledge.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build a Solid Foundation">
    Establish core understanding of cybersecurity concepts and practices across multiple domains including networking, system security, and threat detection.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Gain Hands-On Experience">
    Complete daily exercises using real-world tools and scenarios to develop practical skills that employers value.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Prepare for Certifications">
    Develop the technical skills necessary for industry-recognized certifications such as CompTIA Network+ and Security+.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Key Security Domains">
    Deep dive into critical areas including network security, system hardening, cloud security, scripting, and ethical hacking.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Cultivate Consistent Learning Habits">
    Build a sustainable learning routine over 90 days to support long-term retention, growth, and continuous improvement in your cybersecurity journey.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
By the end of this journey, you should feel confident navigating a variety of cybersecurity tools, concepts, and techniques.
</Note>

## Who This Program Is For

This 90-day cybersecurity challenge is designed to accommodate learners from diverse backgrounds and career stages.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Aspiring Cybersecurity Professionals" icon="graduation-cap">
    Perfect for individuals preparing for entry-level security roles or working toward foundational certifications like CompTIA Security+ and Network+. This structured path will give you the knowledge and hands-on experience needed to start your cybersecurity career.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="IT Professionals in Transition" icon="arrows-turn-to-dots">
    Ideal for system administrators, network engineers, and IT support specialists looking to pivot into security-focused careers. Leverage your existing technical knowledge while building specialized security skills.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Students" icon="book-open">
    Great for students studying computer science, information systems, network engineering, or related fields who want practical, industry-relevant security knowledge to complement their academic studies.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Self-Learners" icon="person-chalkboard">
    Designed for motivated individuals who prefer structured, comprehensive study plans with clear daily objectives. No classroom required - just dedication and curiosity.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Developers and DevOps Engineers" icon="code">
    Valuable for software developers and DevOps professionals who want to better understand secure infrastructure, threat detection, and security best practices to build more secure applications and systems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Curious Tech Enthusiasts" icon="lightbulb">
    Open to anyone curious about how cybersecurity works in real-world environments, from protecting networks to detecting threats and responding to incidents.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Prerequisites

<Tip>
No prior cybersecurity experience is required! However, basic familiarity with computers, networks, or programming will be helpful as you progress through the material.
</Tip>

**Helpful but not required:**
- Basic understanding of how computers and networks work
- Familiarity with command-line interfaces
- Some exposure to programming concepts
- Curiosity and willingness to learn through hands-on practice

**What you'll need:**
- A computer with internet access
- Ability to install software and virtual machines
- Approximately 1-2 hours per day for study and practice
- Commitment to consistent daily learning over 90 days
